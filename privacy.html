Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Demonstration Project Notice

stdy.io is an educational demonstration project built for a Fundamentals of Web Technology course. It uses your browser's LocalStorage as a demonstration database — there is no real backend server, and no data ever leaves your device.

What We Store

Account details, course progress, forum posts and simulated payment records are stored only in your browser's LocalStorage and SessionStorage. Clearing your browser data or using a different device will remove or hide this information.

What We Do Not Do

We do not transmit your data to any server, use real payment gateways, or send real emails. Please do not enter real personal or financial information anywhere on this demonstration site.

Your Choices

You can reset all demonstration data at any time from the Administrator Dashboard, or by clearing your browser's site data manually.
